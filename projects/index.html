Branches Piece

Start: 4.19.2020
End: 4.24.2020

I have this idea for an art piece for above my couch on the wall. There is this space that I have been really wanting to fill, but I don’t really want just any piece that I can get off the shelves. I want something truly special. When I was five my parents had the opportunity to buy some land outside of the city [Springfield, MO]. This is where they got the privilege to build the home I grew up in. This land is the real privilege though; 14 acres of pure natural Splenda. So, in the spirit of this little section of earth I grew up on I want to take a branch and section it into 3 parts and three frames. The three parts symbolize my two siblings and I splitting apart taking what we have learned from the trunk, my parents, and building our own frame, or family. I wish to construct this with my father while we both have free time together, and I think it will make a beautiful piece to show to the people who visit me in my little world/apartment.

[UPDATE 4.24] The project is mostly complete! Just waiting on the glue to harden completely, but all in all I think it turned out fantastic! My father picked out a beautiful branch, and the frame was not without some flaws from the stain clumping a bit, but it ended up gorgeous. Super happy with how it turned out and I cannot wait to see what visitors think.

Portfolio Website

Start: 3.25.2020

I have kinda been itching to start a site for a while now. I wasn’t quite sure what I wanted it to be, but I was afraid my skills in web dev were going to wither and with me probably joining the CS career market, I figured ok. I have more free time now than ever with having to shelter in place, and how hard can joining back in the ranks of web dev be. Oh my oh my, how I have forgotten how hard it is to design something with css. I decided to start with a simple floating header and it took me a few days but, I am glad to say, my skills have been renewed. I do plan on keeping the site up to date. I am very satisfied with the progress I have made, though!

I do still have goals for this project. First I do want to optimize the website more, like clean up my css and get rid of stuff I do not need. I also wanna make sure there is no responsive design issues. One of the goals with the site was to create a place for my writing, I am really proud of how my blog turned out, now I just have to keep pace and keep adding entries. Lastly I want to make sure my site is as secure as it can be. Although the site is quite static and no data is really being transferred I want to see if I can make any more security improvements other than using an encrypted SSH. Plus who knows what the future holds, maybe an infinite scrolling blog list, individual blog pages per post, or hopefully better photos (a photo with a horse maybe?) who knows… the possibilities are endless!!
