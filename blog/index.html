Quarantine Thoughts

4.4.2020

I do not think as humans we are ever satisfied. I do not know what kind of monkey's paw I used but right before all this quarantine stuff happened I was so very burned out at work. Although I truly do miss my cowokers, I honestly think this time I have been given is the silverist of linings. Even though I got laid off, I have been given so much time with my parents that I did not ever think would happen again post moving away for school. I was thinking the other day about how people always say to enjoy the now, but I don't think many ever heed that warning. I am choosing to make it a conscious goal to really enjoy this time with my family because I think I will look back at this time with very sweet memories given awful circumstances.
