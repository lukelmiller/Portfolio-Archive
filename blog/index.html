My Smart Home

4.7.2020

As I am staying with my parents to save money and spend time with them during quarentine, there a few things I miss about my apartment, two of which being, the internet and my HomeKit setup. First off, the internet, it's so helpful. I don't know if you've ever used it but its like a place where you can do everything with the push of a button, truly amazing stuff. Then my smart home, even though it is controlled with a HomePod, and Siri is mostly garbage, being able to say something into the air and have things change in your home is next level stuff. I have smart-ified most of the things: the lights, the doorbell, the thermostat, locks, the vaccum, the humidifier, speakers, and the alarm system. When think of something new I want for my apartment, I first think of how it can make my life easier through automation. Not only because automated devices are cool, but also extremely convenient. Being away from all my cool tech has made me really miss tinkering and designing my spaces around it. One of the things I've been wanting to put in my space are the new Ikea smart blinds. I think it super futuristic to able to automate my natural lighting to wake me up with the sun, while having privacy while I sleep. These are just some of the really neat things I can do and wish to do with my smart spaces in the future.

To get a bit meta, with this blog I really wanted to kinda get my feet wet talking about my smart home. This subject is one I that really doesn't overlap with any of my friends so this blog and reddit are my only ways I can talk about my it and so this avenue is something I have wanted to create for a while, without really knowing the best way of going about it. So thank you for reading and I hope you look forward to more writings about this to come.

Quarantine Thoughts

4.4.2020

I do not know what kind of monkey's paw I used but right before all this quarantine stuff happened but I was so very burned out at work. Although I truly do miss my cowokers, I honestly think this time I have been given is the silverist of linings. Even though I got laid off, I have been given so much time with my parents that I did not ever think would happen again post moving away for school. I was thinking the other day about how people always say to enjoy the now, but I don't think many ever heed that warning. I am choosing to make it a conscious goal to really enjoy this time with my family because I think I will look back at this time with very sweet memories given awful circumstances. For this time I never knew I wished for, I am greatful and happy.
