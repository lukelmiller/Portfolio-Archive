My Smart Home

4.7.20

Whenever I have guest over, I am always excited to show off my smart home. My entire apartment is made up of HomeKit devices from the lights all the way down to my air purifier. I have been building out my smart home for a few years now and it still brings me joy to be able to shout out into the air to control nearly everything in my home. Where the real magic comes in is through automation. My home is on a schedule to wake me, get my morning going, lock up everything once I leave, and turn the lights on and unlock the door once I get home. Whenever I am thinking about new devices for my home, I try to think of how I can fit it into my automated life. As my life moves on and I eventually grow into a bigger house I will try to keep this blog updated as to some automations that seem to appreciably make my life easier.
